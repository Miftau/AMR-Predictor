Our Mission

To combat antimicrobial resistance through innovative AI solutions that provide accurate, explainable, and accessible prediction tools for healthcare professionals worldwide.

Fighting the Global Health Crisis

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is one of the most pressing global health challenges of our time. Traditional laboratory methods for determining antibiotic susceptibility can take days, delaying critical treatment decisions and contributing to the spread of resistant infections.

Our platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to predict antimicrobial resistance patterns rapidly and accurately, enabling healthcare providers to make informed decisions faster and improve patient outcomes.
